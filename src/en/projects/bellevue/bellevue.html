BERLIN-CENTER

House Bellevue



Gross Floor Area: 6.300 square meters
Investment Volume: € 21 Million
Type of Use: Apartments
Architect: Oswald Mathias Ungers
Size of the Project Team: 7 Employees

Hühne Development Services was also responsible for project development for the Berlin-based House Bellevue, which is located in a prime location in Berlin between Unter den Linden and Spree - from conception through project management for the planning and construction phase to marketing and sale. Our goal was to combine the advantages of an old building with modern living comfort.

In the middle of an office-like environment, the elegant clinker façade with wooden windows and bay windows made it possible to convey the character of a residential building.

Floor-to-ceiling windows, high-quality materials and floor plans, reminiscent of well-cut old-style apartments, make the actual living comfort. In addition, there are many technical details that follow the guiding principle that any technology should work as inconspicuously as possible.

The House Bellevue is the convincing result of a planning and awarding process in which everything superfluous has been dispensed with in order to allow enough flexibility for the important, quality-determining components.
