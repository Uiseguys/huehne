BERLIN-CHARLOTTENBURG

City Light



Gross Floor Area: 12.500 square meters
Investment Volume: € 45 million
Types of Use: Offices, Retail
Architect: Collignon Fischötter

Between Kurfürstendamm and Gedächtniskirche in Berlin City - West lies the City Light House. The project was initiated by a subsidiary of Landesbank Hessen-Thüringen. As a joint venture partner, we participated in the construction project and took on part of the project development tasks.

As the project was successfully sold to Credit Suisse before the foundation stone was laid, the lease was already taken by the new owner.
