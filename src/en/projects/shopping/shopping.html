FRANKFURT AM MAIN

Shopping-Center



Gross Floor Area: 90.000 square meters
Types of Use: Retail, Catering, Entertainment
Project Idea: Matthias C. Hühne
Architect: The Jerde Partnership
Masterplan: Michael Beye, Lukas Scheid
Size of the Project Team: 8 Employees

In this project, Hühne Development Services came up with the idea for an inner-city lifestyle shopping center, whose radiance and appeal extends far beyond the borders of Frankfurt: Here shopping should become an extraordinary experience.

As a basis for the planning, we first defined - based on the formal language of an urban planning master plan - an annular, two-storey shopping arcade along an open piazza.

With The Jerde Partnership of Santa Monica, USA, we have been able to gain an architectural office that has proven, around the world and under a variety of conditions, how to transform a creative and thought-out architecture into a magnet for tenants and visitors alike.
