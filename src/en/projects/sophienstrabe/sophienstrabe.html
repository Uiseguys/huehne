BERLIN-CENTER

Sophienstraße 1



Gross Floor Area: 26.500 square meters
Investment Volume: €80 Million
Types of Use: Offices, Retail, Apartments
Architect: Oswald Mathias Ungers
Size of the Project Team: 9 Employees

The concept for the Dorotheenhöfe arose in a difficult, oversupplied market environment. In order to successfully realize a building complex of high quality here, we had to develop a product that matched exactly to the expected demand.

With Oswald Mathias Ungers, we were able to win the ideal architect for our project. In order to give companies the opportunity to buy individual houses, we defined a concept with five differently sized buildings and a common underground car park.

Thanks to intensive preparatory work, we were able to successfully sell all office buildings to renowned companies even before the foundation stone was laid. Our project team ensured a complete completion on time and on budget as well as punctual handover of the individual buildings to their new owners.
