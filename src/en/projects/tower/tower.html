FRANKFURT AM MAIN

Tower 1



Gross Floor Area: 95.000 square meters
Investment Volume: € 450 million
Types of Use: Office
Architect: Jean Nouvel
Size of the Project Team: 8 Employees

The Tower 1, designed by the renowned French architect Jean Nouvel in the best inner-city Frankfurt location, we accompanied from the conception to the building application and detailed building planning.

In the form that we have developed and can be approved and implemented, Tower 1 has the potential to set standards on the Frankfurt office market: a prestigious building that also offers maximum space efficiency and user-friendliness.

The building design is the result of the close collaboration with the architect Jean Nouvel, various specialist planners and authorities as well as the evaluation of a detailed investigation of all comparably high Frankfurt skyscrapers.

Highest comfort in the offices ensure a well thought-out interior, openable windows and a clear ceiling height of 3 meters. The Skygarden on the top floors offers meeting and presentation rooms with a magnificent view over Frankfurt and the surrounding area.
